History of Margherita Lotti

Growing up barely above the poverty line, Founder Aileen Sinay sourced her passion for service from her humble beginnings.

In 2008, she led a career transition program and helped re-employ more than 2,000 employees after the closure of Intel Philippines.

She was able to help people find new jobs, setup businesses, and retire actively.

Margherita Lotti is the actualization of Aileen’s dreams to continue turning grief into hope one job at a time.
