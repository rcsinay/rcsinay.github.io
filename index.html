Meet Margheritta Lotti

We are a search and recruitment company. Founded in 2016, Margherita Lotti works to link career seekers to the best possible position that would deliver afruitful relationship Beyond the Infinite.

We are your partner

At Margherita Lotti, we treat career seekers as clients and partners by providing them with expert bespoke career advisory services underpinned by over thirty years of human resource management experience. Moreover, our services are free to our client career seekers.

We give you the best opportunities

We connect the best people, their talents, skills, career and life goals and aspirations, with the best organizations. We are committed to help candidates find “Beyond the Infinite” relationships by putting them in a position where they can achieve, and at the same time, enjoy.

We are specialized for hard-to-find talents

We specialize in finding hard-to-find talents unobtainable to others, as well as consulting engagements implausible to others, hence, we aptly named our company by the birth name of St. Rita of Casia, known as the Patroness of Impossible Causes.

Now let me tell you how it all started.

Margherita Lotti Founder and Managing Director, Aileen Sinay, has always been passionate about helping people ever since childhood, coming from humble beginnings and growing up in San Andres Bukid district of Manila where most lived barely above the poverty line.

In 2008, Aileen acted as one the lead consultants and project managers in the career transition program for more than 2,000 employees displaced by the closure of Intel Philippines. This program helped re-employ 38% locally, setup businesses for 25%, find jobs abroad and retire actively for the rest. The fulfillment of turning grief into hope and leading people to better careers extending to the families of these people was just a great inspiration, that Aileen has dedicated the rest of her career to developing and transitioning careers with consulting and search firms.

Desiring to help more careers and with a better way of finding and placing talents, Aileen founded Margherita Lotti in 2016. After less than a year in operation, the Company has helped transition more than 30 careers with a wide range of positions from hard-to- find specialized buyers to top chief operating officers.
